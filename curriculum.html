requirements

  1. Music exercise book A4 (2)
  2. Single ruled exercise book A4 (2)
  3. Blue, black biro pens(2)
  4. Sharp pencil(2)
  5. The AB music theory guide text book by E Tailor (optional)
  6. Smart phone/laptop for online classes

    7. students undertaking instrument lessons are advised to purchase their own for practice purposes

pam assessments

Exams shall be administered upon competion of each grade. Both practical and theory exams will be administered

End grade 5 ABRSM exam shall be administred at the ABRSM examination centre in kenya. all students have to be physically present for the exam.

mode of learning

physical

conducted once a week in the learning centers at the stipulated times according to the class time table. Students are advised to stricly adhere to the rules and regulations.

Online Learning

This is conducted twice a week according to the time table. Private students wil make local arrangements with their facilitators. virtual learning platforms such as google meet, zoom are used together with pictorials, you tube videos recorded voices/videos, arranged notes and practice questions, both theory and practical.
